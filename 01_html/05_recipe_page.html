Paneer Tikka Masala

Paneer Tikka Masala is a popular North Indian dish known for its rich, creamy, and flavorful gravy combined with smoky, marinated paneer (Indian cottage cheese). It’s a vegetarian favorite and often served in restaurants alongside naan, roti, or basmati rice.

Ingredients

  • Paneer
  • Yoghurt
  • Tomato puree
  • Onion, garlic, ginger

Instructions

  1. Marinate the paneer: Mix yogurt, chili powder, turmeric, cumin, garam masala, ginger-garlic paste, and a little oil. Coat paneer cubes and rest for 20–30 minutes.
  2. Cook the tikka: Grill, pan-fry, or air-fry the marinated paneer until slightly charred.
  3. Make the base: Sauté onions, ginger, and garlic in butter; add tomato purée, chili powder, coriander, and garam masala. Cook until thick and glossy.
  4. Enrich the gravy: Add cream (or cashew paste) and kasuri methi; simmer to develop flavor.
  5. Combine: Add the cooked paneer tikka to the gravy, simmer a few minutes, and serve hot with naan or rice.
